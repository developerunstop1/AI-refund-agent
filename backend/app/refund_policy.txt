AI REFUND POLICY DOCUMENT

1. Customers may request refunds within 30 days of purchase.

2. Refunds are NOT allowed for:
   - Gift cards
   - Downloadable software
   - Final sale items
   - Digital subscriptions
   - Customized products

3. Refund requests above $500 MUST be escalated
   to a human supervisor.

4. Orders marked as fraudulent are automatically denied.

5. Refunds require:
   - Valid order ID
   - Valid customer ID
   - Matching customer ownership

6. Repeat refund abuse may result in account suspension.

7. Products damaged intentionally are not eligible.

8. Refunds for used wearable items are denied.

9. Shipping fees are non-refundable.

10. AI agent decisions are logged for audit review.
